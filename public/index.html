Three steps. You'll be chatting with the bot in under a minute.

Privacy

Last updated April 20, 2026.

What we collect

Just the essentials: your email, an optional display name, a hashed password, the routes you save, and the alerts you schedule. If you link Telegram, we store your chat ID so we can send you pings.

What we don't collect

We don't track your location, read your device ID, or sell anything to anyone. There are no third-party analytics, no ad networks, no fingerprinting.

Where your data lives

On our servers, in a Postgres database. CTA arrival data is requested on-demand from the Chicago Transit Authority's public APIs — we don't proxy your identity to them.

Deleting your data

Sign in → Profile → change password or unlink Telegram anytime. To fully delete your account and everything in it, email hello@ctatrack.app and we'll wipe it within 7 days.

Cookies

A single session token stored in your browser's localStorage. That's it. No third-party cookies.

Terms

Last updated April 20, 2026.

Use at your own risk

On the Loop surfaces arrival times from the Chicago Transit Authority's public APIs. The data can be late, wrong, or missing. Don't use it as your only signal for time-sensitive trips.

Not affiliated with CTA

This is an independent project. We are not the Chicago Transit Authority, we don't speak for them, and we don't guarantee their data.

Fair use

Don't scrape us. Don't use the service to send spam. Don't try to break things. If you do, we'll cut off your access.
